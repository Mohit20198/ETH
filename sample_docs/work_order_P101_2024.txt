Equipment Tag: P-101

Location: Process Area - Unit 3
Equipment Type: Centrifugal Pump
Manufacturer: KSB Pumps
Model: Etanorm 65-200

Failure Description:
Pump P-101 reported excessive vibration (vibration level: 12.5 mm/s, alarm setpoint: 7.0 mm/s)
and high bearing temperature (bearing temperature: 89°C, normal operating: 45-55°C).
Pump tripped on high-temperature protection at 14:32 hrs.

Root Cause (preliminary):
Mechanical seal failure leading to process fluid ingress into bearing housing.
Suspected root cause: dry running during low-level condition on suction vessel V-101
on 12-March-2024 (refer incident report IR-2024-031).

Work Performed:
1. Isolation and lockout/tagout completed as per SOP-MAINT-004
2. Impeller inspection: pitting corrosion noted on impeller eye (20% material loss)
3. Mechanical seal replaced (Part No: KSB-SEAL-65-V2)
4. Bearings replaced: Drive-end bearing 6313-C3, Non-drive-end bearing 6311-C3
5. Shaft alignment checked and corrected: offset 0.03mm (within tolerance 0.05mm)
6. Seal flush system flushed and refilled with fresh demineralized water
7. Post-maintenance vibration test: 3.2 mm/s (within normal range)

Regulatory Reference: OISD-RP-167 Section 4.3 - Centrifugal Pump Maintenance

Supervised By: Anand Singh (Senior Mechanical Engineer, Employee ID: ME-0089)
